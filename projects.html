Hardware · UMN Rocket Team

Rocket Avionics & GNC

GNC & Avionics lead work on the UMN Rocket Team. Designed a custom backplane PCB in Altium using the CAN bus protocol, debugged with a logic analyzer. Built a Simulink flight state estimator using sensor fusion for flight stage detection — converted to C via Simulink codegen and embedded into the UFC flight computer. Also designed a LoRa avionics bay PCB for telemetry.

Analog Circuit Design Labs

Series of analog design labs: variable-gain and summing amplifiers with intermodulation product analysis (Lab 3), LM741 differential amplifier in audio and thermocouple configurations (Lab 4), and a treble tone-control amplifier with full Fourier and frequency response analysis (Lab 5). All documented in LaTeX with Python-generated plots.
